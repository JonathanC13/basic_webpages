Heartbreak

The end of the first generation - NA Miata passed the torch to the second generation - NB. A great car, but it had removed a feature that initially hooked me to the NA, the pop up headlights were no more. I understand the reasons why they were removed, like less mechanical parts that could malfunction, cheaper to install, and safety regulations. As for safety regulations, how much safer is it being annihilated by a flat front super pickup than a short hood with pop ups.

The current generation - ND, does look cool, but a tad too aggressive for my taste. By other peoples' accounts comparing NA vs ND driving performance and driver feel, like steering, suspension, and gearing, the NA did most better, but I cannot confirm or deny.
