Love

The NA Miata, my dream car. At first glance it's a small and sporty 2 door convertible that can have a hard or soft top, but most importantly; Look how dang ✨ cute ✨ it is! Pop up headlights and charming smile. The 1990 debut's heart is a 1.6-liter four-cylinder that produces 116 horses at 6500 rpm and 100 lb-ft of torque at 5500. With a weight of 2,116 lbs, the amount of pure SPEED that is experienced just feels right. As the old proverb states, it's a heck more fun to drive a slow car fast than a fast car slow. The drivetrain is rear wheel drive to achieve that sweet, approximately, 50/50 weight distribution from head to toe and its 5 speed manual transmission grants full control of this beast. All in all, it's a cheap, reliable, easy to work on, lighweight, and fun car.

Now, as I mentioned, the NA Miata is my dream car. I've never owned one, never rode in one, worked on one, and haven't driven a manual car before outside of video games. I may never be able to own any model of Miata, but I can admire them from a distance and let out an audible "ooh" when I see them in the wild.
